About IDR

WHERE
RIGOUR
MEETS
PRACTICE.

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute specialising in digital, cyber and technology risk. We exist to close the critical gap between theoretical knowledge and the operational realities of high-consequence, regulated environments.

Through a UK university partnership, IDR delivers programmes built on cutting-edge research and validated by senior industry practitioners. Every graduate is deployment-ready on day one — equipped with the frameworks, tools and professional judgement that organisations in financial services, critical infrastructure and beyond demand.
